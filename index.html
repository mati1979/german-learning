German Learning Journey

A realistic, intensive roadmap from A1 to B2 — designed for deep learning, writing-based drilling, and real-world German (Working Holiday & PiA-ready).

How This Course Works

⏱ ~10–11 months · 4h/day · Writing-first · No fluff

  • Grammar is learned inside sentences, not in isolation
  • Writing is the main tool for automation
  • Accuracy comes after sentence control — not before
  • No locked content: progress is tracked, not blocked

A1 · Beginner

4 weeks ~100 hours

Build sentence control first, then refine accuracy. Survival German that actually sticks.

  • Verb position (V2) and sentence skeletons
  • sein / haben / regular verbs
  • Nominative & accusative in context
  • Modal verbs & separable verbs
  • Basic questions, negation, imperatives
  • ~800–1000 high-frequency words

A2 · Elementary

7–8 weeks ~180 hours

Turn structures into habits: cases, past narration, and simple complex sentences.

  • Dative case & two-way prepositions
  • Perfekt (past tense)
  • Reflexive verbs & verb–preposition combos
  • weil / dass / wenn clauses
  • Comparatives & basic adjective endings
  • Total vocab: ~2000 words

B1 · Intermediate

12–14 weeks ~350 hours

From knowledge to independence: fluency automation under real speed.

  • All four cases solidified
  • Präteritum, Plusquamperfekt, Futur
  • Passive voice
  • Konjunktiv II (hypotheticals)
  • Relative clauses & connectors
  • Functional writing & discussion skills

B2 · Upper Intermediate

16–20 weeks ~400 hours

Nuance and professional German: complex structures, register, and PiA-ready language.

  • Konjunktiv I (indirect speech)
  • Advanced passive & alternatives
  • Nominalization & dense written German
  • Academic & bureaucratic language
  • Argumentation, reports, professional emails
  • PiA / workplace-specific vocabulary
Built for consistency, not perfection. Progress beats intensity.
