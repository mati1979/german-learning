Personalpronomen (Personal Pronouns)

The German Pronouns

These are the building blocks of every sentence! Memorize these first.

German Pronunciation English Usage
ich ish I talking about yourself
du doo you (informal) friends, family, children, pets
er air he masculine person/thing
sie zee she feminine person/thing
es ess it neutral person/thing
wir veer we group including yourself
ihr ear you all (informal) group of friends/family
sie zee they group of people/things
Sie zee you (formal) strangers, bosses, elders - ALWAYS CAPITALIZED!
‚úø important: Notice that "sie" has THREE meanings! Context tells you which one:
‚Ä¢ sie = she (lowercase)
‚Ä¢ sie = they (lowercase)
‚Ä¢ Sie = you formal (ALWAYS capitalized, even in the middle of sentences!)
üåø cultural tip: Use "Sie" with anyone you don't know well, work colleagues, teachers, shop workers, doctors, etc. Wait for them to offer "du" before switching! It's a sign of respect.

Pronoun Conjugation Pronunciation English
